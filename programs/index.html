Our programs

Little steps, joyful learning

Purposeful, play-based experiences for young children at every stage of their early learning journey.

Pre-primary only

Learning made for little learners

We offer only three age-appropriate programs: Nursery, Junior KG, and Senior KG.

🧸

Nursery

A gentle introduction to school through play, sensory exploration, stories, music, movement, colours, shapes, and social interaction.

  • Guided play and exploration
  • Stories, songs and movement
  • Social and emotional growth
🔤

Junior KG

Developing early language, number awareness, communication, creativity, coordination, independence, and classroom habits.

  • Early language and number awareness
  • Creative expression
  • Confidence and independence
🔢

Senior KG

Strengthening foundational literacy, numeracy, confidence, problem-solving, communication, and school readiness through engaging activities.

  • Foundational literacy and numeracy
  • Problem-solving through play
  • Happy school readiness

For enquiries about any program, please contact the school directly.
